Policy
Library

Ready-made policy scaffolds for popular MCP servers. Each file lists every tool grouped by category — Read, Write, Execute, Financial, Destructive. Copy one, add your rules, and enforce.

cp policies/stripe.yaml policy.yaml
# add your rules, then:
intercept -c policy.yaml --upstream https://mcp.stripe.com
